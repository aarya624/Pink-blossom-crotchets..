Basics Stitches

Now if this is your first time crocheting, I will be teaching you the basics of single, double, and even half double crochet. However, if you have crocheted before, you can either skip a few pages or do a little recap. Now, let us start with the single crochet. If you do not know how to start, please watch the video and then start single crochet.


Single Crochet:

  • Chain x 10
  • Put your hook through the chain
  • Yarn over again
  • Pull your hook out of the chain (This should leave you with 2 loops on your hook)
  • Yarn over again
  • Pull through both the loops
Ta-da! That was easy! Was it not? Now repeat this process until you run out of chains and there you have it, a row of single crochets!
